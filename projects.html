Projects

Here are some of the projects I have worked on:

Joystick Interface for Virtual Reality (VR) Box

A joystick interface was made with the help of microcontrollers like Arduino UNO which is compatible with Raspberry Pi 4, works as an alternate for mouse or trackpad.

Tools: Arduino UNO, Raspberry Pi 4

Crash Detection System

A sensor interface circuit which can track and miniute changes in an automotive system which could trigger an warning if an accident or any other mishaps take place.

Tools: Arduino Nano, ESP32, Sensor Circuit

VR Development for Teaching of Dhatuposhana Nyaya

An Initiative taken by a team of 6 Members for Smart India Hackathon - 2023 Software Edition where a virtual reality box was created for students who are training in ayurveda to learn about the Ayurvedic term called Dhatuposhana Nyaya

My Experience: We the team was selected as a finalist, where we had an oppurtunity to visit the prestigious institution Prin. L.N Welingkar Institute of Management Development and Research we showcased our talent and completed the project within the quoted time of 48 hours
